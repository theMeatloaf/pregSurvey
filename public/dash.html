Daily Music:

Click below to choose which song you would like to listen to today. Make sure you make a comfy place for yourself, turn off your alerts on your phone so nothing might disturb you. When you’re ready, make yourself really comfy - you have this time for yourself. Become aware of your breathing. Life can be busy, complicated and hectic, so take a moment to turn your attention inwards, to yourself and to your breathing. Be aware of the sounds around you. And when you're ready, play one of the songs - allow yourself to just be. Thoughts will come and go – just let them pass. It doesn’t matter if an unwelcome thought comes, or a good one. Just let each thought or feeling be exactly what it is - without judgment. Acknowledge what comes, and allow it to pass. As the songs draw to a close, take the time you need to rest and just be. And when you're ready, gently return to the room, and to your day.

Below you will find 8 different listening sessions. You do not need to listen to them in a specific order. Feel free to choose any of the sessions on any given day. You can choose a different one each day or stick to a favourite.

Questionnaires:

This is where you'll take the questionnaires when they are due. Please take them as soon as possible after they have become available.

The questions within the survey will ask you about how you are feeling in general and about your pregnancy and baby. There will also be other questions about you in general and about certain other things that may be going on in your life. Each questionnaire will take about 30-40 minutes in total so make sure you leave enough time to fill in the full questionnaire.
